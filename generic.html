I always did well in all my writing classes, and poetry was most interesting because of the way how it incorporated what I learned, and expressed the creativity of my mind. It is a way to be creative within certain boundaries, which forces the user to become cleverer in the crafting of each line. Poetry is a passion that truly brings joy to me, and every good poet must read a lot to fuel the mind. Knowledge is very powerful, and something I take very seriously. The knowledge I gain from reading, I use to improve all aspects of my life.
